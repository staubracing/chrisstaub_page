The Essentials

I’m Christopher—deeply engaged in manufacturing engineering and passionate about motorcycle racing. My day job keeps me focused on process reliability, compliance, and continuous improvement for life-saving medical devices.

Beyond the plant floor, I thrive on the precision and adrenaline of racing. Training, testing, and race weekends teach me discipline, awareness, and how to stay calm when every second counts.

Music (Tool, Primus, Frank Zappa, and plenty of jazz) fuels creativity, while the Dune mantra “Fear is the mind-killer” keeps me centered. I’m committed to staying physically and mentally sharp so I can keep pushing forward on every front.
